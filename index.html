PROJECT OVERVIEW:

The U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) created the RE-Powering America’s Land Initiative to demonstrate the enormous potential that contaminated lands, landfills, and mine sites provide for developing renewable energy in the United States. Here is a RE-Powering Mapper tool to show spatial database of more than 75,000 sites that have been evaluated for renewable energy potential.
